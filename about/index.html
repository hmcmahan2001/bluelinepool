About Blue Line Pool & Spa

Aggie-Owned. Family-Built. Locally Run.

Aggie-Owned. Family-Built. Locally Run.

Blue Line Pool and Spa is owned and operated by Kyle McMahan, a Texas A&M student who took the reins of the family business while still in school. What started as a way to earn his way through college has become his calling and his full-time commitment to the Bryan-College Station community that raised him up as an Aggie.

Kyle runs Blue Line the way Aggies do everything: with hustle, integrity, and a handshake that means something. Every pool he services, every spa he installs, and every customer call he returns carries the same standard — do the job right, do it on time, and treat people the way you'd want your own family treated.

What You Can Expect

We are a family of Aggies serving a community of Aggies, neighbors, and friends. When you call Blue Line, you're not getting a call center or a franchise — you're getting Kyle, his crew, and a company that lives and works in Bryan-College Station.

Gig 'em, and thanks for trusting a local Aggie with your backyard.
