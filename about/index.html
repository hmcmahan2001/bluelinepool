About Blue Line Pool & Spa

Family-owned, Aggie-owned, and built on showing up — every week, on schedule, with the same technician you met the first day.

A family business serving the Brazos Valley

Blue Line Pool & Spa is a family-owned pool service company based in Bryan-College Station, Texas. We serve homeowners across the Brazos Valley — Bryan, College Station, Navasota, Caldwell, Hearne, and Madisonville.

We built Blue Line because pool service in BCS was broken. Route techs who rotate every month. Chemistry "reports" that never arrive. Equipment diagnoses that always end in "you need a new pump." We wanted to do it differently — small team, same tech every week, photo reports after every visit, flat-rate pricing, and the willingness to tell you when a $40 part fixes your problem instead of a $3,000 replacement.

What makes us different

  • Same technician every week. You'll know your tech, and they'll know your pool.
  • Photo reports after every visit. Chemistry, what we added, what we saw — emailed before we leave.
  • Flat-rate pricing, chemicals included. No surprise upcharges.
  • Honest equipment diagnosis. We'll tell you when a part fix is cheaper than a replacement.
  • Local, not a franchise. Decisions are made by the people who answer your calls.

Certifications & membership

We're active members of the pool & spa professional community, and our techs are trained on the equipment brands most common in Bryan-College Station: Pentair, Hayward, Jandy, Polaris, and Raypak.
