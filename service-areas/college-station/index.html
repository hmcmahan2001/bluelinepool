What makes College Station pools different

Hard water & calcium scale

BCS tap water is hard. Without weekly chemistry management, you'll see white calcium scale on tile, cloudy water, and reduced heater efficiency within a few months. Our weekly program includes calcium hardness testing and a documented scale-prevention approach using sequestering agents when needed.

Oak pollen & tree debris

March and April absolutely crush your skimmer baskets in College Station. We come prepared — and during peak pollen weeks, a single weekly visit isn't always enough. Some clients add a mid-week skim for $35 during pollen season.

Summer heat & UV

College Station's summer UV index regularly hits 10+. That means fast chlorine burnoff and rapid CYA (stabilizer) requirement shifts. We manage CYA aggressively to protect your chlorine investment.

New construction & saltwater systems

The new-build neighborhoods (Castlegate II, Greens Prairie Reserve) are heavy with saltwater systems. We service salt cells, replace them at end of life, and know the common equipment lineups from the major BCS pool builders.
