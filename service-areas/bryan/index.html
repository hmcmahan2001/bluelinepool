What makes Bryan pools different

Hard water & calcium

Bryan's tap water runs hard — we see calcium hardness readings of 250-450 ppm right out of the hose. Without active weekly management, you'll see scale on tile lines and reduced heater efficiency in under a year. Our weekly program includes calcium hardness tracking and sequestering agent when conditions call for it.

Oak & pecan debris

Established Bryan neighborhoods like Copperfield and Austin's Colony are beautiful — and they absolutely bury pools in oak pollen, catkins, and pecan debris from March through May. Skimmer baskets fill twice as fast. We come prepared with the right tools and extra time on your property during peak weeks.

Saltwater systems in the newer builds

Traditions, Miramont, and Stonebridge are heavy with saltwater systems. We service salt cells, monitor cell amperage, and replace cells before they cascade into failure. We also know the common equipment lineups from local builders — so diagnostics are fast.

Summer heat & UV

Bryan's summer UV routinely hits "extreme." Without aggressive CYA (stabilizer) management, you'll burn through chlorine by mid-week. We manage stabilizer targets specifically for the Brazos Valley climate.
