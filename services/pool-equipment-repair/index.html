Frequently asked

Pool Equipment Repair FAQ

Do you charge a diagnostic fee?

Yes — we charge a flat-rate diagnostic fee that's waived if you proceed with the repair. We'd rather be upfront than surprise you.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs (pump motor, salt cell, pressure switch) take 1-2 hours on-site. Complex issues may require a follow-up with parts on order.

What brands do you service?

All major brands: Pentair, Hayward, Jandy, Polaris, Raypak, Sta-Rite. If it's in a BCS backyard, we've worked on it.

Do I need a permit for pool equipment work?

Most repairs don't, but replacements involving gas lines or electrical panels may. We'll tell you upfront and pull permits when needed.
