What's included

Every visit, every week, every detail

Weekly service is the backbone of a trouble-free pool in the Texas heat. Brazos Valley summers push chlorine demand, pH, and calcium hardness out of range fast — skipping a week is how problems start. Here's exactly what we do on each visit:

  • Full surface skim — leaves, pollen, and debris off the water line.
  • Brush walls, steps, and waterline tile to prevent algae and calcium buildup.
  • Vacuum floor and benches — manual vacuum, no relying on your robot.
  • Empty skimmer and pump baskets every visit.
  • Full chemistry test — free chlorine, combined chlorine, pH, alkalinity, calcium hardness, CYA, and phosphates.
  • Chemical balancing — all standard chemicals included in your flat rate.
  • Equipment inspection — pump, filter, heater, salt cell, and automation checked visually each visit.
  • Photo email report — chemistry readings, what we added, and photos of your pool before we leave.

Why weekly (not every other week) in Bryan-College Station

Some national chains will sell you bi-weekly service at a lower price. In Bryan-College Station, we won't — and we'll tell you why. With our summer UV index, heat, and humidity, chlorine burns off fast and phosphate levels from pollen spike. By day 10 a bi-weekly pool is trending toward green. By day 14 you're paying for a shock treatment. Weekly service costs less over a year than playing catch-up.

Weekly pool cleaning FAQ

How often should my pool be cleaned in Bryan-College Station?

Weekly is the right cadence for Bryan-College Station pools. Our summers are brutal on pool chemistry — high heat, heavy UV, pollen bursts, and storm runoff push chlorine demand and pH out of range fast.

How much does weekly pool cleaning cost in Bryan-College Station?

Our weekly plans are flat-rate and chemicals are included. Pricing depends on pool size, surroundings, and equipment — request a free quote and we'll get you a custom number within 24 hours.

Do you include chemicals in your weekly service?

Yes — all standard chemicals (chlorine, pH balancers, alkalinity balancers, calcium hardness, CYA, algaecide as needed) are included in the flat monthly rate. No surprise upcharges.

Will I have the same technician every week?

Yes. We're an Aggie family-owned business with a small, trained team. You get the same technician on your pool every week — consistency and accountability, not a rotating crew.

Do I need to be home for weekly service?

Nope. Most clients are at work when we arrive. As long as we have gate access, we'll handle everything and email you a photo report before we leave.

Do you offer saltwater pool cleaning?

Absolutely. We handle saltwater, chlorine, and mineral systems. We also service salt cells and can replace yours when it reaches end of life.
