Pool inspections in Bryan-College Station

Buying a home in Bryan-College Station with a pool? Don't walk into a $15,000 surprise. We do thorough pre-purchase pool inspections — equipment, structure, plumbing, chemistry, and decking — with a written report and photos you can take back to your realtor or the seller.

What's included

Everything that's part of the service

  • Equipment inspection — Pump, filter, heater, salt cell, automation — tested and rated.
  • Structure check — Plaster condition, tile, coping, visible cracks, and signs of shell movement.
  • Plumbing pressure test — We verify suction and return lines hold pressure — leaks are expensive.
  • Chemistry baseline — Full panel reading so you know what shape the water is in.
  • Deck and safety review — Decking condition, drain covers (VGB compliance), fencing and code items.
  • Written report with photos — Emailed within 24 hours. Clear enough to use in negotiation.
Frequently asked

Pool Inspections FAQ

How long does a pool inspection take?

Usually 60-90 minutes on-site, plus report writing. Typical turnaround is 24 hours.

What does a pool inspection cost?

Flat-rate pricing that scales with pool complexity. Request a quote and we'll confirm before we book.

Will you inspect spa-pool combos?

Yes. Attached spas are inspected as part of the combo, no extra fee.

Can you work with my realtor's timeline?

Yes — we work inspections into option periods and can usually be on-site within 2-3 business days.
